There are lot of great pomodoro timers available online but I wanted to customize one with some features that I would personally use. I wanted the tomato icons to pop up to show overall productivity for the session. I'm currently in the process of adding a log, and down the line I'd like to add a calendar as well.

This is written in vanilla JS, and HTML5/CSS.
